Limited Scan Protocol for Mitral and Aortic Valves
# PLAX
* check for effusion
## mitral valve
* MV 2D qualitative assessment - thickening, diastolic doming, hockey stick
* MV regurgitation - colour flow doppler
  * VC < 3mm = mild MR
  * VC > 7mm = severe MR
  * eccentric jet - at least moderate MR
  * **posterior severe MR + peri-arrest may suggest SAM/LVOTO**

## aortic valve
* AV 2D qualitative assessment - calcification, restriction
* <span class="tappable-ref">MACS</span><span class="hidden-ref">maximal aortic cusp separation, >1.2cm unlikely severe AS</span></p>
* regurg - colour flow doppler
    * quantification:
<details>
  <summary>measure VC neck width</summary>
  <div>
    <p>&bull; VC < 3mm = mild AR</p>
    <p>&bull; VC > 6mm = severe AR</p>
  </div>
</details>


<details>
  <summary>LVOT:jet width ratio</summary>
  <div>
    <p> < 25% = mild AR</p>
    <p> 25-65% mod AR</p>
    <p> > 65% severe AR</p>
  </div>
</details>



# PSAX
* mitral valve planimetry - 2D trace of maximal MV opening area
<details>
  <summary>valve area</summary>
  <div>
    <p> >1.5cm2 - mild MS or normal</p>
    <p> 1-1.5cm2 - mod MS</p>
    <p> < 1cm2 - severe MS</p>
  </div>
</details>


# Apical 4/5 chamber view
## mitral valve
* MV 2d - as per PLAX
* MV regurg - as per PLAX
* MV <span class="tappable-ref">mean pressure gradient</span><span class="hidden-ref">continuous wave Doppler through mitral valve and trace valve inflow envelope (E and A wave)</span></p>
<details>
  <summary>MPG</summary>
  <div>
    <p> < 5mmHg - mild MS/normall</p>
    <p> 5-10mmHg - mod MS</p>
    <p> > 10mmHg - severe MS</p>
  </div>
</details>

## aortic valve

* LVOT <span class="tappable-ref">VTI</span><span class="hidden-ref">pulsed wave Doppler with gate 5-10mm from AV valve in LVOT</span></p>
* AV <span class="tappable-ref">mean pressure gradient</span><span class="hidden-ref">continuous wave Doppler through AV and trace the outflow envelope</span></p>
<details>
  <summary>MPG</summary>
  <div>
    <p> < 20mmHg = mild AS (assuming normal LV function)</p>
    <p> 20-40 = mod AS</p>
    <p> > 40mmHg = severe AS</p>
  </div>
</details>
