Standard Echo Protocol
* Confirm patient ID. Acquire height, weight and blood pressure.
* Ensure quality ECG trace is available.
# Parasternal Long Axis (PLAX)
## Left Ventricle
* 2D overview at increased depth - screen for cardiac/pleural effusion
* 2D overview optimized at appropriate depth
* 2D measurements: IVSD, LVEDD, PWD
* eyeball systolic function, size, RWMA

## Aortic Valve
* LVOTd and leaflet opening
* Color Doppler - aortic valve
* Aortic root measurements: Trans-sinus, Sinotubular junction, Ascending aorta

## Mitral Valve
* subjective appearance, doming, hockey stick etc
* Color Doppler for regurgitation

# Parasternal Short Axis (PSAX)
## Mid pap view
* eyeball systolic function
* assess for RWMAs

## Mitral Valve Level
* Color Doppler MV
* can do valve area by planimetry

## Aortic Valve Level
* Color Doppler over AV- regurg jet
* Pulmonary valve - colour, CW doppler
* tricuspid valve - colour, CW doppler (optimised for TR Vmax)

# Apical Views
## Apical 4 Chamber (A4C)
* Atrial volume measurements
* mitral valve - Color Doppler
* PW Doppler mitral inflow (E, A, Decel Time)
* PW Doppler pulmonary venous flow
* TDI: Septal E’, Lateral E’, RV S’
<p>&bull; <span class="tappable-ref">TAPSE</span> <span class="hidden-ref">(- M-mode through the lateral part of the tricuspid annulus, <17mm suggests dysfunction)</span></p>
* tricuspid valve - Color Doppler
* continuous wave Doppler tricuspid valve (TR Vmax)
* 2D zoom LV ± MOD LV volume
* RV measurements

## Apical 5 Chamber (A5C)
* 2D zoom aortic valve 
* Color Doppler
<p>&bull; <span class="tappable-ref">LVOT VTI</span><span class="hidden-ref">pulsed wave doppler with gate in LVOT approx 5mm from AV leaflets</span></p>

## Apical 2 Chamber (A2C)
* LA volume measurement
* mitral valve - Color Doppler
* 2D zoom LV ± MOD LV volume

## Apical Long Axis (APLAX)
* Color Doppler – mitral valve
* Color Doppler – aortic valve
* PW Doppler LVOT (LVOT VTI)
* CW Doppler aortic valve (AoV VTI)

## Apical SAX
* 2D overview
* PEDOF from apical window (if needed)

# Right Sternal Edge View
Use when aorta appears dilated in PLAX or if aortic stenosis suspected
## RSE - Aorta LAX
* 2D overview
* Measurement mid ascending aorta
